About Golf Tempo

Golf Tempo helps you practice swing rhythm with customizable timing, voice prompts, audio cues, and Apple Watch haptic feedback.

  • Train the classic 3:1 golf swing rhythm
  • Customize timing for backswing and downswing practice
  • Use voice prompts, imported sounds, and audio cues
  • Practice with iPhone or Apple Watch

Common Questions

Can I customize the tempo?
Yes. You can adjust timing and cues to match your own practice style.

Can I use my own sounds?
Yes. Golf Tempo supports custom audio cues for your training routine.

Does it work with Apple Watch?
Yes. You can use wrist haptics to feel tempo cues during practice.
