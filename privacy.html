Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

Golf Tempo does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal data, usage data, location data, health data, contact information, or device identifiers.

Your tempo settings, custom timing, voice prompts, and imported audio files stay on your device unless you choose to manage them through Apple system features such as iCloud or device backups.

Third-Party Tracking

Golf Tempo does not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or tracking SDKs.

Apple Watch

If you use Golf Tempo on Apple Watch, haptic and audio cues are used only to support your tempo practice. We do not collect any Apple Watch data.
